Styling & Event Design

My heart is to create unique events that reflect my uniquely individual clients. I'll assist you in determining the overall aesthetic of your event, create mood boards to clearly articulate aesthetic goals, provide vendor recommendations based on desired aesthetic, communicate with vendors to ensure that all aesthetic elements stay true to the vision, collaborate with other vendors to achieve your design goals, and serve as the main point of contact for all aesthetic details day of.

Planning & Production

A beautiful event means nothing if you're not able to enjoy it with your friends and family. I will curate a comprehensive production schedule that communicates all details, coordinate with and manage your vendors, and be on site to direct event flow day of.

Coordination

I will provide guidance and direction throughout the planning process, monitor your progress, create budget projections, make informed vendor referrals, evaluate and negotiate vendor agreements, and act as your advisor, troubleshooter, and event producer.
