Kelly Jones Events was birthed from a longing to create beautiful, unique experiences for people to share. The events, or experiences, that I create are a picture of something greater - lots of people from different walks of life coming to take part in something bigger, greater than themselves. Seeing community form and art experienced in the form of an event satisfies a certain desire. Creating a space for people to experience something together, enter into community together, and see what big dreams little people are able to dream up, is what I love most about event planning.
